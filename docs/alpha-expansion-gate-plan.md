# Alpha Expansion Gate Plan

Milestone 24 adds the source-controlled alpha expansion gate:

- `unreal/Content/RaftSim/Automation/alpha_expansion_gate.json`
- `physics/reports/milestone24/alpha_expansion_gate.json`

The gate exists to stop content growth from outrunning validation. It is a blocking control point for all playable alpha expansion.

## Required Lanes

Alpha promotion requires:

- Automated content validation for river selection, systems progression, crew conversation, production coverage, network gates, and source policies.
- Physics regression replay against accepted water, analytic guardrail, feature/raft, live-water smoke, and deterministic replay evidence.
- River-source provenance checks for source manifests, CRS, transforms, flow bands, license/terms, rights, field media, and guide-review metadata.
- Playable end-to-end river-section runs from selection through launch, completion/failure, restart, scoring, replay review, and after-action feedback.

## Promotion Status

The gate contract passes, but alpha promotion is not open yet. South Fork is the first promotion candidate once the gate lanes pass. Colorado and Pacuare remain planning-only until their reviewed source, hydrology, geospatial, protected-area, guide, and field-media blockers are closed.
