# Futaleufu Canopy Strategy Review

Written July 15, 2026. This is the required strategy checkpoint after the Cordilleran-cypress authoring loop reached 47 locked reviews with no production promotion. It changes no capture, review JSON, generator, environment authority, or gate threshold.

## Recommendation

Pause new in-repository cypress variants and run a bounded **hybrid sourcing pilot**:

- use a dedicated vegetation authoring tool for ecology-reviewed hero and near-bank specimens;
- evaluate a rights-cleared scanned or library analog for distant canopy mass only if the owner accepts an ecology-reviewed analog in place of an exact species;
- keep the project's existing temporal, silhouette, photometry, corridor, hazard-readability, and performance gates unchanged;
- expand to the required adult/intermediate forms and companion species only after one adult form passes the complete gate stack.

This is not permission to buy or import an asset. License, commercial-use, source-file, redistribution, platform, and collaborator rights must pass `docs/free-and-ai-asset-policy.md` before acquisition.

## Stop-loss rules

1. **Freeze v44.** Do not create another in-repo cypress variant until the owner selects a sourcing strategy and ecology-analog policy.
2. **One-form pilot.** Spend no more than six implementation/review cycles on one adult form before the next strategy checkpoint: source/provenance, shape/material, temporal handoff, lit corridor, cleanup/hazard framing, and packaged performance.
3. **Improvement floor.** A representation iteration must improve the active failing silhouette IoU by at least `0.02` or reduce absolute fixed-overlap luminance-ratio error by at least `0.05`. Two consecutive sub-floor iterations end that representation path.
4. **No family multiplication before promotion.** Do not build five adult forms, three intermediates, companion species, or the Zambezi family until one adult form passes source cleanup, front/back shape and photometry, temporal handoff, named-hazard framing, and measured desktop/VR budgets.
5. **Five-iteration checkpoint.** Every fifth asset iteration triggers a written source-strategy review rather than an automatic next variant.
6. **Rights fail closed.** Missing commercial/platform rights, source-file entitlement, redistribution constraints, or acquisition provenance ends the candidate before import.
7. **Gates do not move to rescue an asset.** Threshold changes require independent evidence that the gate is wrong, not evidence that a candidate repeatedly fails it.

## Owner decisions

The recommended next decision is: **authorize a hybrid pilot and allow an ecology-reviewed analog for distant strata only, while requiring custom or exact-species hero/near-bank specimens.** Procurement remains separate and must name the tool/assets, current cost, license terms, and rights record before purchase.

If the owner rejects ecology-reviewed analogs, the fallback is a dedicated vegetation-authoring pilot for a custom exact-species approximation. Continuing the existing hand-rolled generator without a strategy change is not recommended.
