# Custom Field Recording Plan

## Goal

Capture signature rafting audio that commercial libraries cannot provide: guide-seat perspective, real rapids at multiple flows, raft-specific contact, paddle mechanics, hydrophone layers, ambisonic beds, and crew reactions.

## First Target

Start with the validated South Fork American River / Chili Bar to Coloma content package, then add other river types after the workflow is proven.

Flow targets:

- Low runnable: exposed rocks, shallows, slower water, training ambience.
- Median runnable: default commercial flow, balanced rapid beds, paddle teamwork.
- High runnable: stronger hydraulics, wave trains, spray, urgent crew and rescue states.

## Perspectives

- Stern guide seat.
- Bow.
- Mid-raft passenger.
- Shore/scout.
- Eddy.
- Rapid feature close pass.
- Underwater/hydrophone.
- Canyon-wall reflection.
- Rescue/swimmer perspective.

## Capture Kit

- Ambisonic microphone for canyon/forest/river beds.
- ORTF/XY stereo for guide-seat and shore beds.
- Spaced stereo for wide rapids.
- Close mono for raft impacts, paddle catches, gear, rope, pumps, helmets, PFDs, and footsteps.
- Hydrophone for underwater/near-water transitions.
- Timecode or slate workflow for camera/reference sync.

## Safety And Permissions

- Use professional river safety support.
- Record only from safe positions or controlled raft runs.
- Confirm access, permits, release schedules, weather, water level, and land-management rules.
- Record performer consent for all crew reactions, calls, panic, laughter, exertion, and dialogue.
- Keep the audio team out of rescue-critical roles.

## Session Metadata

Each take must record river, section, rapid, flow/gauge, weather, season, position, mic setup, recordist, date/time, take id, safety notes, and intended Unreal playback context.

The canonical Unreal-facing plan is `unreal/Content/RaftSim/Audio/custom_field_recording_plan.json`.
