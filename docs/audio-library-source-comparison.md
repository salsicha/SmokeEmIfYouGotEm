# Audio Library Source Comparison

## Recommendation

Use a blended source stack:

1. **BOOM Library** and **Pro Sound Effects** for high-confidence commercial backbone purchases.
2. **A Sound Effect** for specialized independent libraries after per-pack license/provenance review.
3. **Soundly** as a search/workflow and supplemental library tool after account/team terms are reviewed.
4. **Fab** only for Unreal-ready packs that pass source, license, and quality review.
5. **Sound Ideas / Sounddogs-style catalogs** for supplemental broad coverage, especially legacy Foley, impacts, crowds, and weather, after license review.
6. **Freesound** only for CC0/CC-BY prototype or carefully tracked commercial assets; avoid NC and unclear/legacy licenses for shipping.
7. **BBC Sound Effects** for reference, research, and prototypes unless separately licensed for commercial use.

## Vendor Matrix

| Source | Best Use | Strength | Risk / Review Needed |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| BOOM Library | Water-adjacent design, weather, impacts, cinematic layers, construction kits | Professional quality, commercial media license language, no attribution for synchronized production use under current terms | Check user count, subscription/perpetual terms, AI/ML restrictions, and whether a pack is BOOM or partner content |
| Pro Sound Effects | Broad production SFX, SoundQ workflow, high-quality general coverage | Strong catalog breadth and production workflow | Confirm license seat/team terms and whether a specific library has extra third-party restrictions |
| A Sound Effect | Indie/niche water, nature, game, Foley, ambience packs | Marketplace breadth and specialty packs | Review each product/vendor, license, metadata quality, update history, and provenance |
| Soundly | Search, audition, team workflow, supplemental library access | Useful tool layer for browsing/local/cloud assets | Terms URL needs manual account-level review before relying on it as a shipping source |
| Fab audio packs | Unreal-ready assets and quick prototypes | Easy UE ingest and game-oriented packs | Review each asset's source provenance, Fab terms, update history, and whether audio is human-made, recorded, synthesized, or generated |
| Sound Ideas / Sounddogs-style catalogs | Legacy Foley, weather, crowds, impacts, reference sounds | Large established catalogs | License pages and product terms vary; review each product before purchase |
| Freesound | Prototype textures and occasional CC0/CC-BY source | Huge community source, visible Creative Commons labels | Avoid NC/unclear licenses; attribution and provenance tracking are mandatory for CC-BY |
| BBC Sound Effects | Research, education, reference, prototyping | Large historic archive | Public archive is not a default commercial shipping source; separate licensing is required |

## First-Pass Ranking

For SmokeEmIfYouGotEm, prioritize:

1. BOOM Library water/weather/impact-relevant packs.
2. Pro Sound Effects broad water, Foley, weather, and ambience coverage.
3. A Sound Effect niche river, raft, paddle, rope, gear, and outdoor ambience packs.
4. Custom field recordings for signature river identity.
5. Soundly for workflow if the team needs search, audition, and metadata tooling.
6. Freesound CC0/CC-BY only for prototypes or explicitly approved texture gaps.
